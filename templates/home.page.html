Welcome to Fort Smythe Bed and Breakfast

You are home away from home. Set on the majestic waters of the Atlantic Ocean. This will be a vacation you will never forget. The Fort Smythe Bed and Breakfast is a historic landmark in the heart of the city. It is a beautiful house with a rich history. The house was built in 1920 by the Smythe family. The Smythe family was one of the most prominent families in the city. They were known for their hospitality and generosity. The house was built to be a place where people could come and stay while they were visiting the city.
